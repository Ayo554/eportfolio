Mid-Module Assignment 1 - Presentation

Assessment Brief
For this task, you will deal with the case of EncroChat hacking and its effect in investigating cybercrime for your country of origin or any other country you choose.
You can start by browsing through the Eurojust website:

  • Dismantling of an encrypted network sends shockwaves through organised crime groups across Europe.
  • 7.2 EncroChat: Dismantling of an encrypted network used by criminal groups.

You will then need to carry out research to create this case study analysis.
The analysis should cover the following:
  • The country chosen and the cybercrime chosen, for which EncroChat could be used.
  • A description and explanation of the chosen cybercrime (type, nature) as well as the way EncroChat could be used.
  • An analysis of the ways the chosen country’s law enforcement could deal with the chosen cybercrime using evidence from hacking EncroChat.
  • If available, the debates and narrative in the legal world, NGOs etc. regarding the use of evidence from the EncroChat hacking.
  • If available, present the issues concerning the use of evidence from the EncroChat hacking for the crime investigation, as they were raised in court decisions (jurisprudence).
A critical examination of public and social perception in general and in your chosen country regarding EncroChat hacking. You can draw evidence from the ‘reader comments’ section in online news reels.
